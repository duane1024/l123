/Print Suspend

/Print Suspend -- Stops a background print job temporarily.
/Print Resume  -- Continues printing a background print job that was
                  temporarily suspended.

Use /Print Suspend when you need to change the paper in the printer
or perform another task that requires interrupting a background print job.

Use /Print Resume to continue printing a background print job that was
temporarily suspended.

1. Select /Print Suspend.
2. Select /Print Resume when you are ready for 1-2-3 to complete the
    background print job.
