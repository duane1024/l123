Date and Time Numbers

Date @functions use date numbers, consecutive integers from 1
(January 1, 1900) through 73050 (December 31, 2099). @DATE, @DATEVALUE,
@NOW, or @TODAY can supply date-numbers for @DAY, @MONTH, and @YEAR.

Time @functions use time numbers, consecutive decimal values
from .0000000 (midnight) through .999988 (11:59:59 p.m.). @NOW, @TIME,
or @TIMEVALUE can supply time-numbers for @HOUR, @MINUTE, and @SECOND.

To format date and time numbers so l123 displays them as actual dates
and times, use /Range Format Date or /Worksheet Global Format Date.
