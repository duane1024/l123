/Print [Background, Encoded, Printer] Options Advanced AutoLf

/Print [Background, Encoded, Printer] Options Advanced AutoLf -- Tells l123
whether to insert a line feed after each printed line. Either l123 or your
printer must insert a line feed after each line to print properly. Use this
command to override the /Worksheet Global Default Printer AutoLf setting.

1. Select /Print [B,E,P] Options Advanced AutoLf.
2. Select Yes if your printer automatically inserts a line feed after
    each printed line (so l123 will not insert a line feed). Select
    No (default) if your printer does not insert a line feed (so l123
    will insert a line feed).

If the lines in your printout are on top of each other, neither your
printer nor l123 inserted a line feed. Change the AutoLf setting to
No. If the lines in your printout are double-spaced, both your printer
and l123 inserted line feeds. Change the AutoLf setting to Yes.
