    @PMT(principal,interest,term)
        Calculates the amount of the periodic payment needed to
        pay off a loan (principal), given a specified periodic
        interest rate and number of payment periods (term).
        1-2-3 assumes your calculations are for payments you make
        at the end of each payment period (an ordinary annuity).

    @PROPER(string)
        Converts the letters in string to proper capitalization;
        the first letter of each word uppercase, and the
        remaining letters of each word lowercase.
