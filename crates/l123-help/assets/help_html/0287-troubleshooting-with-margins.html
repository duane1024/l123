Troubleshooting with Margins

If your margins and print range are wider than the paper, 1-2-3 may
print the extra data on the next line, print in one space at the right
margin, or not print at all. Either set a smaller margin and print
range or use compressed pitch (/Print [B,E,P] Options Advanced Layout
Pitch Compressed).

If the print range is wider than the margins, 1-2-3 prints the ends of
lines on another page. To print more characters per line, either
increase the margins or use compressed pitch (/Print [B,E,P]
Options Advanced Layout Pitch Compressed) if your printer can print
compressed pitch.

Use /Print [B,E,P] Options Advanced Layout Orientation Landscape if you
need to print a range wider than the paper and your printer can print
sideways on the paper.
