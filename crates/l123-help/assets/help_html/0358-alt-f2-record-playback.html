ALT-F2 (RECORD) Playback

ALT-F2 (RECORD) Playback -- As you use 1-2-3, the program records your
keystrokes in the record buffer, a 512-byte area of computer memory.
To repeat a task by performing the task once and then playing back
your keystrokes from the record buffer, do the following:

1. Move the cell pointer to wherever you will be starting the task you
    want to record.
2. Press ALT-F2 (RECORD) and select Erase to clear the record buffer.
3. Perform the task you want to record.
4. If necessary, move the cell pointer to the location where you want to
    repeat the task you performed.
5. Press ALT-F2 (RECORD) and select Playback to display the contents of the
    record buffer in the control panel. Edit those keystrokes, if necessary.
6. Select the keystrokes to play back: Move the cursor to the beginning or
    end of those keystrokes; press TAB to anchor the cursor; use the
    pointer-movement keys to highlight the keystrokes; and press ENTER.

As soon as you press ENTER, 1-2-3 repeats the highlighted keystrokes.
