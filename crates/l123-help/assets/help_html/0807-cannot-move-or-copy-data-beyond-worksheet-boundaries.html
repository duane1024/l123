Cannot Move or Copy data beyond worksheet boundaries

The following conditions could have caused this error:

* You selected /Worksheet Insert Row, but the number of rows you
   specified to insert exceeded the number of blank rows at the
   bottom of the worksheet. (A blank row contains no data, formatted
   cells, or unprotected cells.)
* You selected /Worksheet Page, but the last row in the worksheet contains
   data, formatted cells, or unprotected cells, preventing a new row with a
   page-break symbol from being inserted.
* You tried to move or copy data, but the range you specified to copy or
   move occupied either more rows than the total number of remaining rows
   in the worksheet or more worksheets than the total number of remaining
   worksheets in the file.
* You selected /File Combine, but the range you specified to combine
   included more worksheets than the total number of remaining worksheets in
   the file.
