Function Keys

Function Keys -- Perform special operations when you press F1 through F10,
when you hold down ALT and press F1 through F10, and when you hold down
CTRL and press F9 and F10.

Key                Effect
ALT-F1 (COMPOSE)   Creates characters in l123 that you cannot enter directly
                   from your keyboard.
ALT-F2 (RECORD)    Lets you use the contents of the record buffer and turn
                   STEP mode on or off.
ALT-F3 (RUN)       Selects a macro to run.
ALT-F4 (UNDO)      When the undo feature is on, cancels changes made to your
                   worksheet since l123 was last in READY mode.
ALT-F6 (ZOOM)      Enlarges the current window to full-screen size or shrinks
                   it to its original size.
ALT-F7 (APP1)
ALT-F8 (APP2)       - Start an available add-in.
ALT-F9 (APP3)
ALT-F10 (ADDIN)    Displays a menu of available add-in applications.
