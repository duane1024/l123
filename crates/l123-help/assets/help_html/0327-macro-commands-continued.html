Macro Commands (continued)

    {GET location}
        Suspends macro execution until you press a key, then records
        your keystroke as a left-aligned label in location and continues
        the macro. You can press any key except CTRL-BREAK.

    {GETLABEL prompt,location}
        Displays prompt in the control panel and suspends macro
        execution while you type a response. When you press ENTER,
        stores your response as a left-aligned label in location
        and continues the macro.

    {GETNUMBER prompt,location}
        Displays prompt in the control panel and suspends macro execution
        while you type a response, which must be a number or numeric
        formula. When you press ENTER, evaluates your response, stores
        the resulting number in location, and continues the macro.
