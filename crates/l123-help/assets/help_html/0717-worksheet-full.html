One of the following conditions could have caused this error:

* You used a command that copies or moves data, but the range you
   specified to copy or move did not fit within the worksheet
   borders. Make sure the TO range is large enough to hold the data
   you want to copy or move.

* You used /Worksheet Insert Column, but 1-2-3 could not complete
   the command because the number of columns you tried to insert
   exceeded the number of blank columns at the right worksheet border.
   A blank column contains no data, formatted cells, or unprotected cells.
