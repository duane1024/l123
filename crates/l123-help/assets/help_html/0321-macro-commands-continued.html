Macro Commands (continued)
    {BREAK}
        During data entry or selection of a 1-2-3 command, returns
        1-2-3 to READY mode.

    {BREAKOFF}
        Disables CTRL-BREAK, preventing interruption of a macro
        until the macro ends or reaches {BREAKON}.

    {BREAKON}
        Restores use of CTRL-BREAK while a macro is running, undoing
        a {BREAKOFF} command.

    {CLOSE}
        Closes the open text file, if one is open. After executing a
        {CLOSE} command, 1-2-3 goes directly to the next cell in the
        macro. Do not include any further macro instructions in the same
        cell as the {CLOSE} command because 1-2-3 will not execute them.
