Using /Data Fill with Dates and Times

     Date and time start and stop values: Use any l123 Date or Time format
    except Short Intn'l (D5). For example, 14-Nov-93 and 10:30 AM are
    valid start and stop values.

    NOTE  When entering a time for a stop value, specify a time slightly
    greater than the desired last value by an amount less than the step value.

     Date step values: Use a number followed by d, w, m, q, or y to specify
    an increment of days, weeks, months, quarters, or years. For example,
    enter 6w to specify a step value of 6 weeks.

     Time step values: Use a number followed by s, min, or h to specify
    an increment of seconds, minutes, or hours. For example, enter 10min to
    specify a step value of 10 minutes.
