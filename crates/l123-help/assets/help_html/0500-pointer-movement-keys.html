Pointer-Movement Keys

Pointer-Movement Keys -- Move the cell pointer around the worksheet when
l123 is in READY mode. When l123 is in POINT mode, you can use these keys
to move the cell pointer and specify a range in the worksheet.

    or                      Moves up or down one row.
    or                      Moves right or left one column.
CTRL-   or SHIFT-TAB             Moves left one full screen.
CTRL-   or TAB                   Moves right one full screen.
END    or END              Moves right or left to a cell next to a blank cell.
END    or END              Moves up or down to a cell next to a blank cell.
END HOME                 Moves to the lower right corner of the worksheet's
                          active area.
HOME                     Moves to cell A1 in the current worksheet.
PG UP or PG DN           Moves up or down one full screen.
