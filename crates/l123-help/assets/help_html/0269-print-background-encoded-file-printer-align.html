/Print [Background, Encoded, File, Printer] Align

/Print [Background, Encoded, File, Printer] Align -- Resets the page number to
1 so 1-2-3 begins printing at the top of a new page and prints the number of
lines specified in /Print [B,E,F,P] Options Pg-Length. Align ensures that
the headers, footers, graphs, and page breaks will be positioned correctly
when you print.

1. If you are printing on a printer, position the printer paper at the
    top of a page.
2. Select /Print [B,E,F,P] Align.
