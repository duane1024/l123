The input range is A:A1..A:D6. If you specify F1..G2 as the criteria range,
1-2-3 matches all records where Name is Morse. If you specify F5..G6 as
the criteria range, 1-2-3 matches all records where Sales is greater than
1900.

  A:  A          B          C              D       E       F        G

  1  Name       Month    Account        Sales          Name       Sales
  2  Kaplan     May      Theater Inc    1950.00        +A2=Morse
  3  Mintz      June     Dance Corp     1800.00
  4  Morse      July     Art Etc        2100.00
  5  Robinson   May      Travel Log     1700.00        Name       Sales
  6  Smith      June     Photo Show     1850.00                  +Sales>1900
