Operators

Operators -- Use the operators below to create formulas. 1-2-3 performs
operations with lower precedence (Prec) numbers first and performs
operations with equal precedence left-to-right. To override the order of
precedence, enclose an operation in parentheses.

Operator     Prec    Definition                          Example
^              1     Exponentiation                      A1^2
-   +          2     Negative and positive values        +A1, -B1
*   /          3     Multiplication and division         A1*B1, B1/A1
+   -          4     Addition and subtraction            A1+B1, A1-B1
=   <>         5     Equal-to and not-equal-to           A1=50, A1<>50
<   >          5     Less-than and greater-than          A1<50, A1>50
>=             5     Greater-than-or-equal-to            A1>=50
<=             5     Less-than-or-equal-to               A1<=50
#NOT#          6     Not condition 1                     #NOT#A1<50
#AND#          7     Both condition 1 and condition 2    A1>50#AND#A1<100
#OR#           7     Either condition 1 or condition 2   A1=50#OR#A1=B1
&              7     String concatenation                +"Ms."&" "&"Godiva"
