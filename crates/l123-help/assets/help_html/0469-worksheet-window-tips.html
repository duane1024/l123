/Worksheet Window Tips

    To move the cell pointer between two windows (horizontal or vertical),
   use F6 (WINDOW) in READY or POINT mode. To move between windows in
   perspective view, use F6 (WINDOW), CTRL-PG UP, and CTRL-PG DN.

    To expand a window to full-screen size, press ALT-F6 (ZOOM).
   To return a window to its original size, press ALT-F6 (ZOOM) again.

    When you create horizontal or vertical windows, you can change
   the column widths, hidden and displayed columns, and worksheet titles
   independently.

    When you clear horizontal or vertical windows, l123 uses the
   column widths, hidden and displayed columns, and worksheet titles
   settings from the top or left window.
