Protected cell

You were working in a protected worksheet and one of the following problems
occurred:

* You tried to enter or edit data in a protected cell. Before you can enter
   or edit the data, you must unprotect the cell with /Range Unprot or turn
   off worksheet protection with /Worksheet Global Prot Disable.

* You tried to use a command that moves columns or rows, such as /Worksheet
   Delete Column, /Worksheet Insert Row, or /Worksheet Page. Before you
   can do so, you must turn off worksheet protection with /Worksheet Global
   Prot Disable.
